Pencil is a revolutionary stylus for iPad

Pencil is the most natural and expressive tool for getting ideas on Paper

Accessories Reviews · 21.11.13 at 00:29 AM · Reading 5 min

FiftyThree company has been known for a long time due to the development of its applications Paper, which turns the surface of the iPad in real sketch pad. Paper application created with great attention to detail and very closely follows the look and physics of drawing on paper, pens, pencils, brushes.

Paper application is free, allowing you to try out basic functions: a tool for drawing, a number of different colors and a number of albums for drawing. The rest of the brush and palette is sold through built-in purchase.

However, there is one problem: draw your finger is not always convenient, and knowing that the company FiftyThree decided to take the decision into their own hands and create a stylus called Pencil, which turned out just as beautiful as, convenient and most importantly adds experience with the application Paper's full.

"Great tools inspire great ideas. Pencil is the most natural and expressive tool for implementing ideas Paper. Advanced technology combined with beautiful design to keep you in the flow, without having to change tools. With a rubber band, mixing and adaptive definition touch palm Pencil transmits all the creative possibilities in your hands."

Pencil features

Hand touch detection system (Palm rejection). Working with a stylus Pencil Paper application will automatically detect that you touch the surface of the hand and the iPad will not leave an unnecessary footprint on an electronic canvas.

Eraser. The ability to quickly erase as important as the actual drawing. Even made a mistake fix it is not difficult: scroll with two fingers on the screen surface Paper counterclockwise to undo the last action or turning the stylus Pencil erase drawn, as you erase the usual ordinary pencil eraser.

Grind. Working with the stylus, you can accurately draw a thin line, and your skin may be useful to rub painted in soft surfaces, borders and shadows.

Stylish outside, technological inside

Pencil Stylus is made of a single piece of walnut premium and is sold in two versions: brown wood and black aluminum. The device has an electronic stuffing, for example, turning Pencil sensor will transmit information about the application and switch to the eraser mode automatically. The Pencil integrated Bluetooth Smart, which transmits data on the device, thus consumes very little power. Incidentally Pencil need to periodically recharge from a USB port, but it's not hard, just by removing the tip you connect it to your Mac, and after 90 minutes the stylus is fully charged, and one charge of the device can operate for a month or 43829 minutes.

At the tip of a small built-in rod, which defines with the utmost precision the degree of depression. Rod itself is made of 14-karat gold, which provides lightning speed and accuracy of response. The wooden version of the stylus also has a magnet inside for attaching the stylus to the lid or cover Cover Flow Smart Cover for the iPad.

Device support. Pencil will work with all iPad with retina display and all the iPad mini. iPad 2 is not supported.

Delivery. Currently Pencil will be available in the US and Canada in 2-3 weeks.
